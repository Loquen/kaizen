About

I have trained players and coached teams for the past 10 years in California, Finland, and Sweden. I have coached youth players ages 6-18 for various clubs, high school teams, and with individuals. I have worked as an assistant coach at the collegiate level, and have coached at Stahre International Academy in Stockholm, Sweden.

When working with individuals I specialize in technical development, basing my methods on the teachings of Bruno Redolfi of Federazione Italiana Giuoco Calcio. I have extensive training for physical development, working at the Sports Athletic Advancement for 4 years in Santa Cruz. I have certification for athletic development from Solvalla Idrottsinstitut in Helsinki, Finland, and work closely with a strength and conditioning coach here in the Bay Area. Finally, and most importantly, I worked with a sports psychology coach during my own career and I have developed specific strategies to develop a dominant mindset in soccer players.
